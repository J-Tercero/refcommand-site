# Product vision

## Intended users

RefCommand serves high school football officials, crew chiefs, complete crews, newer referees, and local officiating associations.

## Core problem

Preparation is often spread across messages, personal notes, rule books, and disconnected local sources. Officials need a dependable place to prepare without turning game-day work into another administrative task.

## Value proposition

RefCommand brings practical crew preparation, study tools, useful references, and carefully sourced local football context into one focused command center.

## Initial product scope

The public 2026 foundation provides the information architecture and an accessible homepage for pregame, quizzes, rules and mechanics, news, scores, rankings, and resources. It deliberately avoids inventing content where an editorial or data process does not exist.

## Long-term vision

RefCommand becomes a trusted regional platform where officials and associations can prepare together, share consistent crew data, and access high-quality context before, during, and after the season.
