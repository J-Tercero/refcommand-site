# Roadmap

## Phase 1: Public 2026 season foundation

Launch the static public experience, shared navigation, design system, transparent placeholder states, and product documentation.

## Phase 2: Interactive officiating tools

Add a pregame conference builder, reviewed rules quizzes, and curated rules, mechanics, and resource content.

## Phase 3: Accounts, associations, and shared crew data

Introduce optional accounts, association spaces, crew collaboration, and shared preparation data.

## Phase 4: Larger regional platform

Expand local coverage, sourced scores and rankings, editorial workflows, and regional association support.
